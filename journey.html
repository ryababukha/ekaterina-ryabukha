Laravel and PhpStorm

PhpStorm is JetBrains' third-biggest standalone product by revenue. In 2025, we secured two of the most impactful deals for it in years:

1 Partnered with Laravel, the largest PHP framework, making PhpStorm their officially recommended IDE.
2 Made the most popular PhpStorm plugin free and later bundled it with the IDE. The announcement generated 500K+ organic impressions, and plugin adoption grew from 8% to 25% within the first few weeks.

Both initiatives were crucial for retaining segments most prone to churn (SMBs and individuals).
