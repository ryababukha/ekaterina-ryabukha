Laravel and PhpStorm

PhpStorm is JetBrains’ third-biggest standalone product by revenue. In 2025, we secured two of the most impactful deals for it in years:

  • Partnered with Laravel, the largest PHP framework, making PhpStorm their officially recommended IDE. Implemented revenue-sharing referral links, a mechanism that didn’t previously exist at JetBrains.
  • Made the most popular PhpStorm plugin free and later bundled it with the IDE. Previously, only a small fraction of users installed it due to its paid model. The announcement generated 500K+ organic impressions, and plugin adoption grew from 8% to 25% within the first few weeks.

Both initiatives were crucial for retaining the segments most prone to churn due to the rise of AI editors (SMBs and individuals).
